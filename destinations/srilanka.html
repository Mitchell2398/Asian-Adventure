Sri Lanka

The ideal time to visit Sri Lanka is between December to March for pleasant weather, though May to October is perfect for safaris and surfing on the Eastern Coast, especially at the renowned Arugam Bay.

The country boasts pristine beaches like Mirissa, exciting wildlife adventures in parks like Yala, and breathtaking scenic routes through Ella and Nuwara Eliya.

Dive into the flavorful Sri Lankan cuisine and enjoy the hospitality of its friendly locals for an unforgettable journey.
